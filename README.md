# 🚀 Jet vs Alien

**Jet vs Alien** is an action-packed, retro-style arcade game where you control a high-speed jet and defend your territory against an endless wave of aggressive aliens.

## 🎮 Play the Game Now!
You can play the game directly in your browser here:
### 👉 [**Play Jet vs Alien**](https://nndeeshks.github.io/jet-vs-alien/)

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

*   **Move Left**: Use the `Left Arrow` key.
*   **Move Right**: Use the `Right Arrow` key.
*   **Shoot**: Press the `F` key to fire your laser.
*   **Pause**: Click the `PAUSE` button on the screen.
*   **Goal**: Shoot down as many aliens as possible. The game gets faster as you level up (every 10 points)!

## ✨ Features
*   **Dynamic Difficulty**: Speed increases with every level.
*   **Retro Aesthetics**: Neon-inspired graphics and sound effects.
*   **Real-time Scoring**: Track your score and level as you play.
*   **Explosive Effects**: Visual animations for hits and game over.
